FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about staking, IoT verification, and earning rewards on GrowEase.
Contact now Contact now
  • How does GrowEase staking work?
    Users stake crypto to commit to personal goals. Funds are locked and can only be reclaimed after successful proof via IoT verification.
  • What is IoT verification?
    IoT devices track your activity and sign proof events, ensuring that your progress is verifiable and accurate.
  • Can I earn rewards?
    Yes! Successfully completing your goals earns back your stake, GWE tokens, and yield-based rewards shared from pooled funds.
  • What happens if I fail a challenge?
    Failed stakes are pooled into the DAO or distributed to beneficiaries or challenge winners, encouraging accountability and community participation.
  • Is GrowEase safe and secure?
    All staked funds are managed through smart contracts on Solana, and IoT proofs are cryptographically signed, ensuring maximum security and transparency.
